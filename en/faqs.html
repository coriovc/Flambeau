What are the requirements to apply and receive the co-investment from the Argentine state: What are the requirements to apply and receive the co-investment from the Argentine state:

To receive the co-investment from the Argentine state, the requirements are published on the website of the National Ministry of Production. Some of them are:


1. Being a technology-based startup.


2. Have less than 5 years of existence as a legal person.


3. Owning the political control of the venture and its controller at the head of the original entrepreneurs and that it continue for a minimum period of 180 days.

When are the candidate selection periods? When are the candidate selection periods?

The selection of startups is continuous throughout the year.

What is the candidate selection process What is the candidate selection process

It consists of meetings to get to know each other and find together the acceleration areas for your project. Throughout this process, we will accompany you to the presentation of the business in front of the GV investment committee. In case you are selected, we will finish with due diligence and the signing of the legal agreement to start the GV acceleration program.

What is the counterpart of the GV acceleration program What is the counterpart of the GV acceleration program
